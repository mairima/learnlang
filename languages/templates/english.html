Learn Numbers in English

Start with basic numbers and build up to larger figures.

  • 1 – One
  • 2 – Two
  • 3 – Three
  • 10 – Ten
  • 20 – Twenty
  • 100 – One Hundred
  • 1,000 – One Thousand

Grammar Structure

  • Parts of Speech: Nouns, Pronouns, Verbs, Adjectives, Adverbs, Prepositions, Conjunctions, Interjections
  • Tenses: Present Simple, Past Simple, Present Continuous, Future Simple, etc.
  • Sentence Structure: Subject + Verb + Object
  • Questions: Use auxiliary verbs like Do/Does/Did
  • Negation: Use not (e.g., I do not like coffee.)
